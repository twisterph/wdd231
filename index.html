About Me

My name is Pablo Hinojosa. I am 35 years old and originally from Chile. I currently live in Lake Havasu City, Arizona with my family. I have been married to my wife Leah since March 4, 2023, and we have a son named Nathan Blake Hinojosa. Family is one of the most important parts of my life. Currently, I am studying web development and improving my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive web design. I'm scared but excited to see where this journey takes me.
